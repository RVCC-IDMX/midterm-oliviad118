Fun facts:

• Corals are animals not plants.

• Coral reefs need a lot of sunlight to grow.

• Too much heat can severely damage and harm them.

• Coral reefs go back 240 million years. Sheesh! that's a long time.

• Surprisingly they are not only found in warm water. Some are found in depths as deep as 2,000 metres.

Sleeping Patrick Star 😂

•Starfish has no brain or blood.

•There real name is Sea star

•They eat inside out. Their stomach exits their mouth an returns when done. 😮

•Even though it will take some time they can regenerate.

•They can have up to 40 arms.

Little seahorses hiding. We ride at dawn 🐎

•Technically they are fish

•Male seahorses gives birth

•On another note they are horrible swimmers.

•Seahorses don't have stomaches, so have to follow a strict diet.

•There are over 40 different kind of seahorses.

So many Glofishes

•Glofish was first genetically modified in 2001 and became avaliable in the US in 2003.

•They have the ability to absorb light and re-emit light.

•They pass their color onto their offsprings. Which was inherited from their parents.

•There are seven species of Glofish

•Glofish was bred to detect enviornmental pollutants.
